About Us

Welcome to Great Lakes Integral: Cosmic Concern with a Regional Focus.

At Great Lakes Integral, our mission is to help build, connect, grow, develop, and transform the integral community in the Great Lakes region. To meet our mission, we will invite, invent, and invest in a range of formal and informal practices designed to initialize, stabilize, and optimize integral consciousness in the region. We are both daring in our vision and pragmatic in our methods.

Community

Facilitators

Hokyo Joshua Routhier

Michigan

An Integralist living in Ann Arbor, Hokyo Joshua Routhier began spiritual practice over a decade ago. Practicing everything from shamanism to Sufism, he takes his seat in the GLI and Mondo tribes. Hokyo is an ordained priest in the Hollow Bones Rinzai Zen order and a training mental health counselor at Wayne State University.

In addition, he has studied Gestalt therapy for the last five years, specializing in group therapy and mindfulness-based interventions. Hokyo is the head priest of Thunder Drum Zen, a floating sangha, providing teaching and outreach to Integral practitioners and various spiritual communities in southeast Michigan. He also offers one-on-one meditation instruction and mindfulness consultation as the owner atStay Awake Tutoring and Coaching.

M. Ellis Jaruzel II, PsyD

Michigan

M. Ellis Jaruzel II, PsyD is an award winning integral scholar and a psychotherapist in private practice. His clinical and research interests include those related to the intersection of spirituality and psychotherapy especially: adverse events in meditation, nondual psychotherapy, non-symbolic consciousness, and later-stage adult development.

My short-term goal is to bring the awakening that I experience to the mid-Michigan area.
